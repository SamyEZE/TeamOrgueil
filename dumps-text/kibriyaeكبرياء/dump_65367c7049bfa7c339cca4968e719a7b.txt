Arabic[[13]edit]

        [14]Root
   [15]ك ب ر‎ (k-b-r)

Etymology[[16]edit]

   It is doubted that the formation, otherwise paralleled in the name of
   the [17]seventh heaven [18]عِرْبِيَاء‎ (ʕirbiyāʔ) ~ [19]جِرْبِياء‎
   (jirbiyāʔ), is native, since only in [20]Ge'ez the root of [21]ከብረ
   (käbrä) has acquired the meanings of “haughtiness”; [22]كِبْر‎ (kibr,
   “glory, pride, power”) also directly corresponds to Ge'ez [23]ክብር
   (kəbr).

Pronunciation[[24]edit]

     * [25]IPA^([26]key): /kib.ri.jaːʔ/

Noun[[27]edit]

   كِبْرِيَاء [28]• (kibriyāʔ) f
    1. [29]greatness
    2. [30]pride, [31]arrogance, [32]haughtiness
    3. [33]power
