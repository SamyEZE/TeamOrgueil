   2024 Women's Lacrosse Captains

Captains Announced For Pride In 2024

   11/2/2023 11:30:00 AM
     *
     *
     *
     *
     *
     *

   By: Stephen Gorchov

Story Links

   Hempstead, NY - Hofstra University Head Women's Lacrosse Coach
   [170]Shannon Smith announced today a group of five outstanding
   student-athletes who will serve as captains for the Pride during the
   2024 campaign.
   Earning this important leadership role are: [171]Kate Fiola, [172]Brynn
   Hepting, [173]Ashlyn McDonald, [174]Taylor Mennella, and [175]Trinity
   Reed.
   "We are so thrilled with this group of women to lead us this season,"
   commented Smith. "These five leaders will be great role models for our
   entire program and we look forward to their guidance throughout the
   2024 season."
   This marks the second year as captains for Fiola and Mennella, while
   the other three are all first-time captains.
   Fiola, a graduate student from Massapequa, New York, has played in 45
   games for the Pride. She has 25 career points on 16 goals and nine
   assists, while also adding 49 ground balls and 18 caused turnovers.
   Hepting, a junior from Towson, Maryland, has appeared in and started 30
   games and has totaled 42 draw controls, 26 ground balls, and 19 caused
   turnovers.
   A graduate student from Olney, Maryland, McDonald has played in 26
   games with seven starts. She has six ground balls and seven caused
   turnovers.
   Mennella, a graduate student from Smithtown, New York, has played in 50
   games so far in her career. She has 125 points on 82 goals and 43
   assists, while also adding 25 ground balls and eight caused turnovers.
   A senior from Westbury, New York, Reed has appeared in 34 games during
   her career. She has 51 ground balls and 24 caused turnovers, while
   starting 32 games.
