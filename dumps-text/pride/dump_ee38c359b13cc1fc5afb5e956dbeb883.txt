70 Pride Quotes to Inspire Confidence in Yourself

   Stephanie Kirby
   By [9]Stephanie Kirby | Published May 9, 2022

   Are you lacking a little confidence?

   It might be time to get in touch with your pride.

   These pride quotes will inspire you into action.

   What do you think of when you hear the word “pride”?

   Many people think of “pride” as being something bad, as in “pride goes
   before the fall”.

   And while it’s true that pride can become negative if you are too
   caught up in it, it’s also important.

   Having pride in yourself, your abilities, and even your surroundings
   can be motivating towards action.

   When people are proud of where they live, they help take care of it.

   When a person is proud of their abilities, they aren’t afraid to step
   out and put it to use.

   Healthy pride can [10]build up your confidence as shown in these pride
   quotes.

   Check out our most popular quote article, a list of [11]short
   inspirational quotes for daily inspiration.

   Check out our [12]inspirational quotes category page for more
   inspirational life quotes.

Pride quotes on what it means to be proud

   10. “Pride is holding your head up when everyone around you has theirs

What did you learn from these pride quotes?

   Can you feel your confidence growing just reading through the pride
   quotes above?

   Remember, you don’t have to be perfect in order to have pride in who
   you are.

   Stop comparing yourself to everyone else.

   Stop trying to get it all just right until you can feel good about
   yourself.

   Don’t be afraid to appreciate yourself, have confidence, and be proud
   of yourself.

   It doesn’t mean you have to take it too far.

   Use it for motivation to continue growing and developing as a person
   and helping those around you.

   Choose your favorite quote or two and post them up in places where
   you’ll see them throughout the day.
