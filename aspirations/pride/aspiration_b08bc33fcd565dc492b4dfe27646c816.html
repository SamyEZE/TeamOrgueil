Captains Announced For Pride In 2024

11/2/2023 11:30:00 AM

By: Stephen Gorchov

Story Links

Hempstead, NY - Hofstra University Head Women's Lacrosse Coach Shannon Smith announced today a group of five outstanding student-athletes who will serve as captains for the Pride during the 2024 campaign.Â 

Earning this important leadership role are: Kate Fiola, Brynn Hepting, Ashlyn McDonald, Taylor Mennella, and Trinity Reed.Â 

"We are so thrilled with this group of women to lead us this season," commented Smith. "These five leaders will be great role models for our entire program and we look forward to their guidance throughout the 2024 season."

This marks the second year as captains for Fiola and Mennella, while the other three are all first-time captains.Â

Hepting, a junior from Towson, Maryland, has appeared in and started 30 games and has totaled 42 draw controls, 26 ground balls, and 19 caused turnovers.Â

Mennella, a graduate student from Smithtown, New York, has played in 50 games so far in her career. She has 125 points on 82 goals and 43 assists, while also adding 25 ground balls and eight caused turnovers.Â
