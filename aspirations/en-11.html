35 Inspirational Pride Month Quotes Perfect for Your Next Post

These sayings celebrate and honor the LGBTQ+ community.

By Lizz SchumerUpdated: Apr 10, 2023

You're dressed to impress, the #Squad is assembled and now all you have to do is document it for posterity (or at least your Instagram followers). And luckily, with these best Pride month quotes and sayings about the LGBTQ+ community, coming up with a caption will be a breeze.

After all, a well-chosen funny, pithy, meaningful or inspirational quote is perfect for crafting a caption that will have everyone hitting that like button. Even if you're not the photo-posting type, these sayings are ready to be sent to all of your friends as well-wishes and texts come June.

Whether you want to shout out your Pride month excitement on Instagram, put out the perfect tweet to share your feelings about the importance of equality or mail cards to mark the occasion with your partner, chosen family or ride-or-die crew, we've got a quote that will fit the bill. These are our favorite Pride Month quotes from celebrities, LGBTQ+ movies, LGBTQ+ books and pop culture moments.

From thoughtful reflections on how far we've come as a society and the distance we still have to go, to empowering messages that will pump you up for however you plan to celebrate, to silly sayings that will spread the joy that comes with belonging to the LGBTQ+ family, there's a saying out there for every type of Pride. No matter how you're celebrating, we're excited to help.
